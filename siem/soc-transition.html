SOC Transition

SIEM Track Months 7-12

Transitioning the Security Operations Center from RSA NetWitness to Microsoft Sentinel. Covers analyst training, parallel operations, and cutover procedures.

Role Clarification

My Contribution: Ensured platform readiness for SOC transition — all log sources ingested, connectors validated, M365 Defender integrated, built-in detections enabled. Provided technical support during training and shadow operations.

SOC Manager & Training Team: Training curriculum, analyst skill assessments, workflow migration, runbook updates.

Note: This page documents the full SOC transition approach for reference.

Transition Phases

Gate-Based Cutover

Sentinel became primary SIEM only after:

  • All critical log sources onboarded and validated
  • 90+ days of stable telemetry for baselining and tuning
  • Detection coverage validated against RSA
  • SOC analysts trained and comfortable with KQL/workflows

This approach avoided blind spots, validated detections, and transitioned the SOC safely without operational risk.

Parallel Operations Procedures

Console Assignments (XDR-First)

With XDR enabled from Day 1, the SOC uses the unified M365 Defender portal (security.microsoft.com) as the primary interface.

Unified Portal Experience

Primary SOC Interface: security.microsoft.com

With Sentinel integrated into the M365 Defender portal, analysts work in a single interface. XDR incidents and Sentinel incidents appear in the same queue. Azure Portal Sentinel is for administration only.

Alert Deduplication

During parallel operations, both systems will generate alerts for the same events. Procedure:

  1. Acknowledge alert in both consoles
  2. Investigate in the designated primary console
  3. Document findings in Sentinel incident (single source of truth)
  4. Close in both systems with same resolution

Industry Best Practices

SOC Transition Best Practices

SANS SOC Recommendations:

  • Parallel Operations: Run both SIEMs simultaneously for 3-6 months minimum
  • Detection Validation: Verify all critical detections fire before cutover
  • Analyst Training: 40+ hours platform training before go-live
  • Playbook Migration: Update all runbooks and response procedures
  • Metric Baselines: Establish KPIs before transition for comparison

Gartner SOC Modernization:

  • Target MTTD (Mean Time to Detect): <1 hour for critical alerts
  • Target MTTR (Mean Time to Respond): <4 hours for P2 incidents
  • Automation target: 70%+ alerts with automated enrichment
