Hi Team,

As part of our security enhancement initiative, we're updating our data protection tools. Here's what you need to know:

**What's Changing**
We're transitioning from Symantec DLP to Microsoft Purview DLP for better integration with our Microsoft 365 environment.

**What This Means for You**
- You may see new pop-up notifications when sharing sensitive data
- Initially, these are informational only (no blocking)
- Starting [date], some actions may require additional confirmation

**No Action Needed**
The transition happens automatically. Just be aware of new notifications.

**Questions?**
Contact the Security Team at security@revflow.com

Thank you for helping keep our data safe!
