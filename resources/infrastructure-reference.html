5.2 Escalation Matrix

Incident Escalation Path
REVFLOW SOC ESCALATION MATRIX
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SEVERITY 4 (Low) - Informational
├── Owner: L1 SOC Analyst
├── Response: 4 hours
├── Escalation: None required
└── Example: Policy violation, informational alert

SEVERITY 3 (Medium) - Suspicious Activity
├── Owner: L1 → L2 if unresolved in 2 hours
├── Response: 2 hours
├── Escalation: L2 after 2 hours, L3 after 4 hours
└── Example: Failed login attempts, suspicious process

SEVERITY 2 (High) - Confirmed Threat
├── Owner: L2 SOC Analyst
├── Response: 30 minutes
├── Escalation: L3 immediately, CISO within 2 hours
├── Notification: IT Leadership, affected department head
└── Example: Malware detection, data exfiltration attempt

SEVERITY 1 (Critical) - Active Breach / Major Incident
├── Owner: L3 SOC Lead + Incident Commander
├── Response: 15 minutes
├── Escalation: CISO immediately, CEO within 1 hour
├── Notification: Executive team, Legal, PR (if needed)
├── War Room: Teams channel #incident-war-room
└── Example: Ransomware, APT activity, data breach confirmed
