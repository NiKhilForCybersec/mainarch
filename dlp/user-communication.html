Email Templates

Initial Awareness Email

Email Template
Subject: Important: Data Protection Updates Coming

Hi Team,

Starting [DATE], we're upgrading our data protection systems to better protect 
RevFlow and our customers' information.

What's changing:
• New Microsoft Purview DLP will replace Symantec DLP
• You may see policy tips when working with sensitive data
• Initially, you'll just see warnings (no blocking)

What you need to do:
• Complete the Data Handling training by [DATE] (15 minutes)
• Reach out to Security if you have questions

Why this matters:
Our customers trust us with their data. These protections ensure we maintain 
that trust and meet our compliance obligations.

Questions? Contact security@revflow.com

Thanks,
RevFlow Security Team

Block Mode Announcement

Email Template
Subject: Action Required: Data Protection Now Enforcing

Hi Team,

As announced, our data protection system is now actively blocking 
sensitive data from being shared inappropriately.

What this means:
• Emails with customer data to external addresses will be blocked
• Uploads of sensitive files to personal cloud storage will be stopped
• You can request an override if blocked action is legitimate

If you're blocked:
1. Review if the data really needs to be shared externally
2. If yes, click "Override" and provide business justification
3. If unsure, contact your manager or security@revflow.com

Remember: These controls protect our customers and our company.

Quick Links:
• DLP FAQ: [link]
• Request Exception: [link]
• Security Contact: security@revflow.com

Thanks for your cooperation,
RevFlow Security Team

Training Content

Training Module Outline (15 min)

  1. Why Data Protection Matters (3 min) — Customer trust, compliance, breach impact
  2. What's Protected (3 min) — Customer data, financials, PII examples
  3. What You'll See (4 min) — Policy tips, warnings, blocks demo
  4. How to Handle Blocks (3 min) — Override process, when to escalate
  5. Quiz (2 min) — 5 questions to confirm understanding

Help Desk Preparation

ScenarioHelp Desk Response
"I can't send this email"Check if it contains sensitive data. If legitimate, use Override with justification.
"False positive"Document details, escalate to Security for policy tuning.
"Need permanent exception"Complete exception request form, requires manager approval.
"Policy tip is wrong"Screenshot and submit feedback. Security will review.

Executive Communication

Executive Handling

Executives are high-risk for DLP friction. Pre-brief them individually:

  • 1:1 meeting with CISO or Security Lead
  • Explain what they might encounter
  • Provide direct security contact for issues
  • Consider temporary exclusion for critical deal periods (documented, time-limited)
