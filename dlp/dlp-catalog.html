RevFlow DLP Catalog

DLP Track

Complete catalog of RevFlow's DLP components: Sensitive Information Types customized for our Salesforce data, EDM schemas for customer and employee databases, sensitivity labels aligned with our classification policy, and policies mapped to SOC 2/ISO 27001/PIPEDA requirements.

RevFlow Data Landscape

Primary Data Sources: Salesforce CRM (customer data), Workday (HR), NetSuite (Finance), GitHub (Source Code), Confluence/Jira (Internal Docs)

Compliance Scope: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, PIPEDA (Canada), GDPR (EU customers), CCPA (California)

High-Risk Groups: RF-SALES-ENT (customer data), RF-FIN-* (financial), RF-HR-* (employee PII), RF-ENG-* (source code)

├── Marketing materials, public docs, press releases
├── No restrictions
├── Approver: None required
└── Examples: Website content, product docs, job postings

2. RevFlow Custom Sensitive Information Types

2.1 Salesforce & Customer Data SITs

SIT NameDetection PatternKeywordsConfidencePolicy
RF-SIT-SalesforceID Regex: [0-9a-zA-Z]{15}|[0-9a-zA-Z]{18} salesforce, sfdc, account id, opportunity id, contact id High (85%) P1: Customer Data
RF-SIT-CustomerExport Keywords + file pattern (.csv, .xlsx with headers) account name, opportunity amount, close date, stage, pipeline High (85%) P1: Customer Data
RF-SIT-DealPipeline Keywords + currency patterns deal value, ARR, MRR, contract value, renewal, forecast Medium (75%) P2: Revenue Data
RF-SIT-CustomerPII Email + phone + name patterns customer contact, client email, billing contact High (85%) P1: Customer Data
RF-SIT-CustomerList Bulk detection (10+ customer names) customer list, account list, client roster High (85%) P1: Customer Data
PowerShell - Create RF-SIT-SalesforceID
# RF-SIT-SalesforceID - Detects Salesforce Record IDs
# Owner: RF-SEC-GRC-COMP | Last Updated: 2024-12-01

Connect-IPPSSession -UserPrincipalName admin@revflow.com

$RuleXml = @"

  
    
    
  
  
    
      
        
        
      
      
        
      
    
    \b[0-9a-zA-Z]{15}\b|\b[0-9a-zA-Z]{18}\b
    
      
        salesforce
        sfdc
        account id
        opportunity id
        contact id
        lead id
        case id
      
    
  

"@

New-DlpSensitiveInformationType -Name "RF-SIT-SalesforceID" `
    -Description "RevFlow - Salesforce Record IDs (Accounts, Opportunities, Contacts)" `
    -ClassificationRuleXml $RuleXml

5.2 Policy P1: Customer Data Protection (Detailed)

Policy Configuration
POLICY: P1 - Customer Data Protection
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

POLICY SETTINGS:
├── Name: RF-DLP-P1-CustomerData
├── Status: Enabled (Enforcement)
├── Priority: 1 (Highest)
├── Mode: Block with business justification override
└── Owner: RF-SEC-GRC-COMP

CONDITIONS:
├── Content contains:
│   ├── RF-SIT-SalesforceID (instance count: 5+, confidence: 75%+)
│   ├── OR RF-SIT-CustomerExport (instance count: 1+, confidence: 85%)
│   ├── OR RF-SIT-CustomerPII (instance count: 10+)
│   └── OR RF-EDM-CustomerDB match (any searchable column)
│
├── Content is shared:
│   └── With people outside my organization
│
└── Except if:
    ├── Sender is member of: RF-DLP-Executives
    ├── OR Recipient domain is: @siemens.com, @*.siemens.com
    └── OR Content is labeled: "Internal - Partners"

ACTIONS:
├── Exchange:
│   ├── Block email with policy tip
│   ├── Allow override with business justification
│   └── Notify: sender, sender's manager
│
├── SharePoint/OneDrive:
│   ├── Block external sharing
│   ├── Show policy tip: "This file contains customer data and cannot be shared externally"
│   └── Restrict access to: "Only people in your organization"
│
├── Teams:
│   ├── Block sharing in chats with external participants
│   └── Show policy tip
│
├── Endpoint:
│   ├── Audit: Copy to USB, Print, Upload via browser
│   ├── Warn: Copy to network share, Access by unallowed app
│   └── Unallowed apps: Dropbox, WeTransfer, personal OneDrive

NOTIFICATIONS:
├── User notification: Policy tip in app
├── Admin alert: soc@revflow.com (High severity)
├── Incident report: Weekly to RF-SEC-GRC-COMP
└── Sentinel: DLP events forwarded via connector

COMPLIANCE MAPPING:
├── SOC 2 CC6.1: Logical access controls
├── ISO 27001 A.13.2: Information transfer
└── PIPEDA Principle 4.7: Safeguards

Interview-Ready Summary

"At RevFlow, our DLP strategy centers on protecting customer data from Salesforce, employee PII from Workday, and financial data from NetSuite. We built 15+ custom SITs for RevFlow-specific patterns like Salesforce IDs and project codes, plus 3 EDM schemas that refresh daily against our actual customer and employee databases. Policies are scoped by department — Sales gets stricter customer data controls, Engineering has source code protections, Finance has financial data rules. Integration with Defender for Cloud Apps adds session-level controls for our SaaS apps, blocking bulk exports from Salesforce on unmanaged devices."
