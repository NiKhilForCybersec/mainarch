QuestionResponse Approach

"What if you join a client that's a mess?" That's actually the most valuable consulting work ‚Äî helping organizations that need guidance the most. Framework thinking helps prioritize what to fix first.
"How quickly can you ramp up on new environments?" The framework (Business ‚Üí Planes ‚Üí Assets ‚Üí Risks ‚Üí Controls ‚Üí Telemetry ‚Üí Detections) is universal. Specifics change, structure doesn't. I can map any organization within days.
