DLP Issues

Common Microsoft Purview DLP problems and solutions.

Policy Not Triggering

SymptomPossible CauseSolution
Policy shows no matchesWrong location selectedVerify policy applies to Exchange, OneDrive, Endpoints, etc.
Sensitive info not detectedSIT confidence too highLower confidence threshold or use custom SIT
Endpoint DLP not workingDevice not onboardedCheck MDE onboarding status (required for Endpoint DLP)
Policy in test modeNot yet enforcingMove from "Test" to "Turn it on" in policy settings

False Positives

SymptomPossible CauseSolution
Too many alerts for legitimate business dataSIT too broadCreate custom SIT with more specific patterns
Internal documents flaggedNo sender/recipient exceptionsAdd exceptions for internal domains
Test data triggering alertsDev/test content matches patternsExclude test/dev locations or use content exceptions

User Complaints

ComplaintResponse
"I can't send this email!"Review policy match, add override option if appropriate
"My file was blocked"Check Activity Explorer, verify legitimate business need
"Too many popups"Reduce to Warn instead of Block, or tune thresholds

Check DLP Activity

Purview Portal
Microsoft Purview → Data Loss Prevention → Activity Explorer

Filters:
- Date range: Last 7 days
- Activity type: DLP policy matched
- Policy name: [Your policy]

Review matches to identify false positive patterns
